We Deal Pigs!

Here at Guinea Pig Dealers we have been providing the best quality guinea pigs and cages for over 20 years. Founded as a small guinea pig shop in 2002, we have evolved into Peoria's #1 guinea pig pet store. Our pigs are sourced from South America where they are freely bred. They are bred by the most experienced guinea pig breeders which have been breeding the healthiest and strongest guinea pigs for over 200 years.
